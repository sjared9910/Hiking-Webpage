Craggy Pinnacle Trail

Find yourself on a short 1.4 mile out and back trail to the summit of Craggy Dome. This short and moderately steep trail takes you from the Blue Ridge Parkway through a high elevation forest of birch trees and up to the summit where you can experience a beautiful 360 degree view of the Blue Ridge Mountains.

This moderately difficult trail is well walked and has an easy to follow path. The trail is very rocky and steep in sections with many rock steps. The trail has many cutoffs for those who need to take a break on the ascent. Due to the high elevation, high winds and cold temperatures can occur Plan accordingly to weather and season.

Trail Details

Difficulty: Moderate

Length: 1.4 miles out and back

Climb: Moderate

Lowest Elevation: 5660ft

Highest Elevation: 5892ft

Total Climb: 250ft

Starting Point: Craggy Dome Overlook at Blue Ridge Parkway Mile 364

Where to Find

To get to the Craggy Pinnacle Trail, take the Blue Ridge Parkway north from downtown Asheville. The visitor center is located at mile marker 364 and after passing through the Craggy Pinnacle Tunnel, the Craggy Dome parking lot will be on the left.
