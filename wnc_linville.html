Linville Falls via Erwin's View Trail

Linville Falls is perhaps the most photographed waterfall in North Carolina. Find yourself on the Erwin's View trail, a 2.2 mile trail from the top of the waterfall up to numerous overlook points to view the falls. Here you can see the complete falls as it goes falls 90ft into the plunge basin. If your feeling more adventurous, take the 1.2 mile descent down to the plunge basin and view the bottom of the falls.

The Erwin's View overlook trail is an easy trail with some elevation change. This trail located off the Blue Ridge Parkway and features a paved walkway to the top of the falls with no elevation change. The trail to the overlook is a wide, well walked trail and is easy to navigate. The 1.2 mile trip is slightly uphill with many overlook positions to stop at along the way.

Trail Details

Difficulty: Easy

Length: 2.2 miles out and back

Climb: Easy

Lowest Elevation: 3160 ft

Highest Elevation: 3360 ft

Total Climb: 300 ft

Starting Point: Blue Ridge Parkway milepost 316

Where to Find

Linville Falls is located along the Blue Ridge Parkway at milepost 316. There will be a sign directing to the parking lot. The start of the trail is located next to the giftshop.
